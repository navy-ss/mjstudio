Online Stem Mix

Bouncing down your tracks into stems provides a unique flexibility in the mix down stage. Stems can be sent using any online transfer portal. These stems are mixed and mastered according to the given specifications and sent back to the client. This service allows seamless collaboration between artists and engineers regardless of their geographical location.
